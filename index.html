About me

This is Lily Vahdani and I have Masters' in Business Analytics at the University of Houston, C.T. Bauer College of Business. My passions brought me to Business Analytics. I thrive on challenges that require me to get my hands dirty playing with complex sets of data, analyzing them and extracting those hidden patterns that help me “see” the trends that guide businesses through times of uncertainty by identifying operational bottlenecks or spotting growth opportunities.
